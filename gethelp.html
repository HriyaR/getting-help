How can you help yourself


Tell the people around you


It may be awkward or embarrasing at first, but this one step can make a huge difference in your recovery.
You could also talk to someone online at MyStruggles


Join an eating disorder support group


Connecting with other going through the same things as you can help you look at things from a fresh perspective. It also provides motivation for you to try harder.


Develop a balanced relationship with food


Eat when you are truly hungry. Don't restrict your food intake or diet, an occasional cookie or icecream isn't going to hurt. Stick to a regular eating schedule.


Speak to a nutritional counselor


Eating more than you were or eating less than you were doesn't mean that you have recovered completely. A nutritional counselor or dietician will help you incorporate the right foods into your diet.
